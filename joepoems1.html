72.
A GARDEN

I.
In the middle of Roosevelt Island
a garden waits for me
tulips and posies and all the flowers you like
they're all there waiting for me

II.
where is your camera?
snapping the remains of a happy pretty soil
what lens was yours?
that you saw so far where i couldn’t
with tearied eyes

III.
inbetween the flashes i found space to mutter
“ah but i grew up here
in this concrete veined city
what do i know about gardens?
what would someone like me
know about what’s beautiful?"


-Miguel Cortes, 2019
