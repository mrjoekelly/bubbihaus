Bubbi TV is the visual companion to Bubbi Radio.

Hit play, relax, and enjoy a block of clips curated by yours truly.

If you wanna watch in full-screen (which you should, imho) just hit the "pop-out" button on the top right of the player, which will open the ep in a new window. Then once you hit play, you'll be able to hit "full screen" on the bottom right of the player. Simple enough. Probably made it sound more complicated than it is, but blame google drive's weird embedding coding. Cheers.
